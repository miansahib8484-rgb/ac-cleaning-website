Frequently Asked Questions
أسئلة شائعة حول التواصل والحجز
How quickly will you respond to my booking?
During business hours (8am–10pm), we respond to all WhatsApp and form enquiries within 1 hour. For phone calls, we answer immediately. For emergencies submitted via WhatsApp at any hour, you will receive a reply within 15 minutes.
Do you offer same-day service?
Yes — same-day service is our standard across Riyadh. In most cases we can have a technician at your door within 2–4 hours of booking. For emergency breakdowns, we guarantee arrival within 2 hours, 24 hours a day.
Is the diagnosis visit free?
Yes. For all home appliance calls — AC, washing machine, refrigerator, microwave — the initial diagnosis is completely free. You will receive a written quote before any work begins. If you choose not to proceed, you pay nothing.
Do you serve areas outside central Riyadh?
We cover all Riyadh districts — from Al Quds and Al Olaya in the centre to Al Yasmin, Hittin and Al Nakheel in the north, through to Al Shifa and Badr in the south. We also serve Jeddah and Dammam for contract and corporate clients.
