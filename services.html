FAQ · الأسئلة الشائعة

Common Questions

أسئلة يطرحها عملاؤنا

Everything you need to know before booking a service.

How quickly can you come for an emergency repair?
We guarantee a technician at your door within 2 hours for emergency calls anywhere in Riyadh — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week including Fridays and public holidays. For non-urgent bookings, same-day and next-morning slots are usually available.
Do you charge for the initial diagnosis visit?
No — our initial in-home assessment is completely free of charge. Our technician will visit, inspect your appliance, and give you a clear written quote before any work begins. There is no obligation to proceed.
What warranty do you provide on repairs?
All repairs are backed by our 2-year workmanship warranty. If the same fault reoccurs within 2 years of a repair, we fix it free of charge. Replacement parts also carry the manufacturer's standard warranty on top of ours.
Do you use genuine / original parts?
Yes. We only use OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) parts or certified equivalent parts for every repair. Before fitting any part, we will show you the part number and price — you always know exactly what is being replaced and why.
How often should I clean my AC unit in Riyadh?
Due to Riyadh's dusty environment and heavy AC usage (often 10–12 months per year), we recommend a professional deep clean every 6 months — ideally once before summer (April) and once before winter (October). Regular cleaning cuts electricity bills by up to 30% and prevents costly compressor failure.
Can you service my AC if it's a brand you haven't listed?
Yes. Our technicians are trained on over 40 brands and can service virtually any make or model of air conditioner, washing machine, refrigerator or microwave oven. If you're unsure, just WhatsApp us the brand name — we'll confirm availability before you book.
What areas in Riyadh do you cover?
We cover all districts of Riyadh including Al Quds, Al Olaya, Al Malaz, Al Muruj, Al Nakheel, Al Rawdah, Al Yasmin, Al Sahafa, Al Aqiq, Al Rabwah, Hittin, Al Wurud, and all surrounding areas. We also serve Jeddah and Dammam for contracted clients.
