Our Story · قصتنا
Who We Are · من نحن

From One Van to Riyadh's Most Trusted Name

من سيارة واحدة إلى الاسم الأكثر ثقة في الرياض

Air Conditioning was founded in 2006 by two HVAC engineers with a shared frustration — the AC service industry in Saudi Arabia was full of overcharging, poor workmanship and zero accountability. They decided to do it differently.

Starting with a single service van, two technicians and one simple promise — honest work, transparent pricing, and accountability for every job — they built a reputation customer by customer, job by job.

Today we operate a fleet of 20+ fully equipped vans, a team of 45+ HVAC-certified engineers and electronics technicians, and a 24/7 emergency dispatch centre — all still rooted in the same Al Quds District community where we started.

100% Saudi-owned and operated — proud to serve our community first
Every technician factory-trained on the exact brands they service
Transparent pricing, always — written quote before any work begins
2-year workmanship warranty on every repair and installation, in writing

The Principles That Guide Everything We Do

المبادئ التي توجه كل ما نقوم به

Every hiring decision, every training programme, every repair — guided by six principles we have held since day one.

Honesty First
الصدق أولاً

We give you the real diagnosis, real cost and real recommendation — even if it means saying the appliance is beyond economical repair. Honesty is the foundation of every long-term customer relationship we have.

Technical Excellence
التميز التقني

We invest heavily in tools, diagnostic equipment and manufacturer certification so that every job meets the highest possible standard. Every technician is trained before they ever enter a customer's home.

Customer First, Always
العميل أولاً دائماً

Your comfort drives every decision. From the moment you call to the moment we hand over the warranty, every single step is designed around your experience — not our convenience.

Speed & Reliability
السرعة والموثوقية

Breakdowns don't wait. Our 24/7 emergency team and 2-hour response guarantee means you are never left stranded when you need help most. Same-day availability is the standard, not a premium.

Continuous Learning
التعلم المستمر

We run quarterly training programmes for our entire team covering new brands, technologies and diagnostic tools — so our knowledge is always current and our advice is always worth listening to.

Community Commitment
الالتزام بالمجتمع

We are a Saudi company, built by Saudis, for Saudis. We train young Saudi engineers, employ local talent, and reinvest in the communities we serve — contributing actively to Vision 2030 with every job we complete.

Certifications · الشهادات والاعتمادات

Accredited, Licensed & Certified

معتمدون ومرخصون رسمياً

Our certifications are proof of the technical investment we make in every technician, every year — not just certificates on a wall.

Saudi Ministry of Commerce — Licensed

Fully licensed by the Saudi Ministry of Commerce and Investment since 2006. Operating legally, transparently and accountably — CR number available on any request.

HVAC Engineering Certification

All senior technicians hold HVAC engineering certification covering refrigeration cycles, electrical systems, refrigerant handling and full safety protocols for Saudi climate conditions.

Daikin & Mitsubishi Factory Certified

Factory-authorised by Daikin and Mitsubishi Electric to service their complete product range — with direct access to genuine parts, manufacturer technical support and warranty alignment.

Samsung & LG Authorised Service Partner

Authorised Samsung and LG service partner for home appliances in Riyadh. Genuine OEM parts, manufacturer-approved procedures and factory warranty alignment on every job.

Refrigerant Handling Certificate

All technicians hold certified refrigerant handling licences for R-410A, R-32 and R-600a — mandatory for legal refrigerant purchase and use under Saudi environmental regulations.

Electrical & HV Safety Certification

Electronics technicians hold high-voltage safety and electrical work certifications — essential for safe microwave, refrigerator PCB and control board repair inside customer homes.
